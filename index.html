About Our School

Sri Aurobindo Integral Education Centre (SAIEC), Matrubihar, Dharmagarh, Odisha was founded in 1989 to impart education based on the ideology of The Mother and Sri Aurobindo.

  • Our Mission

Our Mission

The School is based on principle of education as stated by Sri Aurobindo in 'A preface on National Education' in 1920.

"The first principle of true teaching is that nothing can be taught. The teacher is not an instructor or taskmaster, he is a helper and guide. His business is to suggest and not to impose."

We believe in consulting the mind in its own growth, working from the near to the far, and helping each child discover their divine potential.

Our Vision

It is an education proper to the Indian soul and need and temperament and culture that we are in quest of, not indeed something faithful merely to the past, but to the developing soul of India.
